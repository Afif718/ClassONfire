Introduction

Nov 25, 2020

When the COVID-19 pandemic forced the education system to shut worldwide and make billions of students out of classrooms, that time the online education system dramatically changed the whole situation. Attending classes from anywhere now super easy for such online platform, this is how the e-learning system cuts down travel, cost, accommodation along with provides safe, flexible, advanced learning system.


As the present world education depends on the e-learning system, the teachers, students with their parents, and educational institutions are trying to cope with the wave; some are wondering seeing the adoption results. Will this continue after the pandemic? How this system will affect students' academic performance?
Besides the Coronavirus pandemic, several factors contributed to the growth of online enrolment, including the following :

  • Younger people are choosing non-traditional education to start and advance in their careers while completing and furthering their education.
  • Severe recession of 2008 has created poor economic situations where people are upgrading/changing their career through online educational/training programs.

A student can now join the classes at home if he wants by contacting the teachers directly and even solving various textbook problems. Moreover, to online lessons, students can use the internet to view and read multiple educational websites. These have been a blessing for many students during this global epidemic.
